Rob's Tips

Don't drink & Drive

It's been said time and time again. However, too many people needlessly lose their lives because of drunk driving. If you plan on drinking, designate a driver, call a cab or call Rob!

Dont Text & Drive

Smart phones are an invaluable tool these days. They're great for communication, navigation, and hours of entertainment. These things can be a major distraction while driving. Distracted driving is dangerous driving. Use hands free devices if you absolutely need to use your phone while driving. Otherwise, take a few seconds to pull over to use your phone. It could save your life!

Lug Nuts

If you have locking lug nuts on your rims, always know where the key is. They are typically stored in the glove box for easy access.

Tire Pressure

Always check your tire pressure throughout the year as temperatures fluctuate.
